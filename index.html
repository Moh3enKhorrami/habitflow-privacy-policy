Privacy Policy

App: HabitFlow — Daily Habit Tracker
Last updated: April 9, 2026

HabitFlow is designed with privacy in mind. All your data stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

1. Data We Collect

HabitFlow stores the following data locally on your device only, in a SQLite database:

  • Habits you create (name, description, icon, color, category, frequency)
  • Daily completions and streak history
  • Reminders and notification schedules
  • Mood logs and habit notes
  • Routines, challenges, and badges
  • App settings and preferences

This data is never transmitted to us or any third party.

2. Permissions We Request

Notifications

Used to send habit reminders at times you configure. All notification scheduling is handled locally on your device.

Microphone & Speech Recognition

Used optionally for voice-based habit logging. Speech is processed on-device using your operating system's built-in speech recognition (iOS Siri / Android Google Speech). No audio is recorded or stored by HabitFlow.

Calendar

Used optionally to sync your habits as events in your device's native calendar app. Calendar data is read and written directly on your device and is not shared with us.

Health Data (iOS HealthKit / Android Google Fit)

Used optionally to auto-complete health-related habits by reading step count, workout, and sleep data from your device's health platform. We only read this data to check habit completion — we do not store or transmit it.

3. Data Backup

HabitFlow supports the following backup mechanisms:

  • iOS: Automatic iCloud backup (handled by iOS, subject to Apple's privacy policy)
  • Android: Automatic Google Play backup (handled by Android, subject to Google's privacy policy)
  • Manual export: You can export your data as JSON or CSV and share it using your device's native share sheet. This export is fully under your control.

4. Third-Party Services

HabitFlow does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or cloud services. There is no Firebase, no tracking SDK, and no remote database.

The only external integrations are native device features you explicitly enable:

  • Device calendar (read/write)
  • Apple HealthKit / Android Google Fit (read only)
  • Device speech recognition (on-device processing)

5. User Accounts

HabitFlow does not require or support user accounts. No registration, no login, no cloud sync.

6. Children's Privacy

HabitFlow does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
